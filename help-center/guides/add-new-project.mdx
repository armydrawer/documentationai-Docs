## Create Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open dashboard">
    Log in at https://docs.akislitsyn.com
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start new project">
    Click the "New Project" button in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill details">
    Enter project name and description. Click "Create".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add documentation">
    Go to your new project. Click "Add Document" to start writing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
